Troubleshooting when things go wrong...

------------------

Are you still connected to EDU or MissionNet (One may be more reliable than the other.)

Are you using Chrome (other browsers may fail)

Is it the right phase for what you're trying to do?

What does the bottom user-feedback say? (This may clue to what is wrong)

Is the action allowed by the rulebook (double check)

If the island didn't capture, make sure you are using a land piece (helicopters don't count)

If the island still didn't capture, move the piece onto the flag position again (same position, no moves taken) to try to update it.

Did you try refreshing (almost always fixes things)

Are you signed into the right team/section (check upper left menu to verify)

------------------

Tips / Other: (things people forget)

------------------

Drag and hold pieces over things to 'pop' them open (islands / carriers / transports)

Planes must end turns on carriers / airfields. (or will be destroyed)

Tankers refuel planes in their square 'on the transition' between combat phase and fortify phase.

Defending planes (who were on a carrier) survive if their carrier is destroyed.

Attacking planes only stay for 2 'rounds' of their 1 allowed combat, and then leave the combat zone.

Destroyers can't be killed from defense bonus if they attack a piece on land (but they leave after 1 round)

Always read the news alerts (they may affect the game in weird ways)

Planes can only enter a battle if they are in the same square as what was selected (can't be adjacent)

Hover the mouse over things to see details about them (like pieces)

If you mistakenly make a move, use the undo-move button in the bottom left corner.
